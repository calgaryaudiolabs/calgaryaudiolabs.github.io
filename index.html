Who We Are

Calgary Audio Labs is an independent audio engineering group based in Calgary, Alberta. We are a collective of passionate developers and sound engineers dedicated to pushing the boundaries of digital audio processing.

Our mission is to democratize plugin creation. We help artists design, build, and distribute their own signature plugins, bridging the gap between creative vision and technical reality.
